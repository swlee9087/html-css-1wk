Growing Tomatoes

About Tomatoes: There’s a reason why tomatoes are the #1 home garden vegetable. The taste of a tomato right off the vine is incomparable to a typical grocery store type. Tomatoes are warm-weather vegetables and sun worshippers!

How long does it take to grow? The exact “days to harvest” depends on the cultivar and it can range from 60 days to more than 100 days.
